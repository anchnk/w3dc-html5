Short Biography

I'm Olivier Martin, 31 years old living in Bordeaux, France.

Work and Job

I work for Orange, a multinational telecom provider. I'm a Phone Operator in a technical center call.

More about my job

Basically i've to fix customer troubles by phone. I'm working at an expertise degree so, most of the time, i'm not dealing with customers directly but rather with colleague that get difficulties to solve problems.
I'm working on all IP based services we offer (like Voip, Tvip and Internet access) as well as mobiles phones. Some examples of my daily work could be :

  • Assign a schedule so that a technician have to go fix an analog phone line.
  • Troubleshoot network difficulties on computers, private networks, routeurs and alikes.
  • Help customers to remove virus, backdoor, trojans, malware and the likes.
  • Troubleshoot system problem on mobile phone or sending bug reports to moblie phone constructors.
  • Inform and assist in depth customers to discover new mobile OS features
  • Troubleshoot communication problems regarding mobiles phones.
  • Diagnose TV's jiter and reset boxes or change them according to the default

It might sound interesting and sometimes is but most of the time it's boring so i'm doing programing at the same time :]

I've studied computer programing at IUT de Bayonne et Du pays Basque. It did not end in a job in pure programing but i've keep playing with tools along the year, especially regarding digital arts.

I've been mainly active in the Pure Data, VVVV, and Processing communities. In 2008, i did a workshop at an event call node08 : this workshop was about realtime communication and behavorial algorithms between generative media. In short and less barbaric term how to generate sound and visual using behaviors algorithms like velvet integration, physics, boids links them together and create some kind of autonomous piece of generative content.
